Author Journey

How it all began

From a girl with notebooks to a published author. Every page of this journey shaped the stories I tell.

2018

The First Notebook

Started writing poems and short stories in a worn spiral notebook. Words were the only language that felt honest enough for what I felt inside.

2020

First Novel Draft

During the long quiet of 2020, I finished the first draft of what would become "Bound by Secrets." 80,000 words in 4 months.

2021

First Publication

"Bound by Secrets" was published and reached 10,000 readers in its first month. The response was overwhelming and beautiful.

2023

Award Recognition

"Under Your Silence" won Best GL Fiction at the Literary Voices Awards. A moment I'll carry forever.

2026

Today & Beyond

Now with 12+ books and readers in 5 languages, I'm still that girl with a notebook — just writing bigger stories now. This is only the beginning.

Behind the Pages

Writing Process

Every book begins as a feeling. Here's how I turn feelings into worlds.

01

Feel It First

Every story starts with an emotion I can't shake. A feeling so strong it demands to be written. I let it simmer.

02

Build the World

Characters come next. I create detailed journals — their fears, their dreams, their voice. I know them before I write a word.

03

Write Freely

First drafts are emotional and messy. I write without filters, letting the characters lead me wherever they need to go.

04

Refine & Breathe

Editing is where I find the real story. I polish, cut, and add until the words feel exactly right — like they always meant to be there.
